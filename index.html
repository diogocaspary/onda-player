▶ Onda Player

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Onda Player ("the app") is a media player that plays media from a playlist (M3U) or provider credentials that you supply. This policy explains what the app does and does not collect.

1. Content

The app does not include, host, provide, sell or distribute any media or content, and it does not come with any playlist. All content is supplied by you (your own M3U playlist or your own content provider's credentials) and is your and your provider's responsibility.

2. Information stored on your device

Your playlist URL (when you use "Add my list"), your viewing history and "continue watching" positions, and basic app settings are stored locally on your device only. You can clear them at any time from Settings or by uninstalling the app.

3. Information processed on our servers

To enable activation and basic functionality, the app sends to our own servers a device identifier and basic technical information (device model, operating-system version, app version), used only to operate and secure the app. If you choose to sign in with your provider's credentials (username, password and code), those credentials are sent to our server to authenticate you and return your playlist. We do not share any of this with third parties and we do not sell any data. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, location or payment data.

4. Network

When you play media, the app connects directly to the source defined in your own playlist/provider. Those connections are governed by the privacy practices of that third-party source, not by us.

5. Children

The app is a general-purpose media player and is not directed at children under 13.

6. Data deletion

Data stored on your device can be removed at any time from Settings or by uninstalling the app. To request deletion of any data associated with your device or login on our servers — without deleting any account — email diogocaspary@gmail.com and we will delete it.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

8. Contact
